Climbing Gear & Equipment

Learn about the equipment climbers use.

Climbing Shoes


  • Special shoes that help climbers grip small holds on the rock.
  • Tight fit for better control.

Harness

  • Worn around the waist and legs.
  • Connects the climber to the rope for safety.

Rope

  • Strong climbing rope used for protection when climbing high.
  • Helps catch climbers if they fall.

Helmet

  • Protects the climber's head from falling rocks or impacts.

Chalk & Chalk Bags

  • Keeps hands dry for better grip.
  • Carried in a small bag on the waist.

Carabiners & Belay Devices

  • Carabiners: metal clips used to connect gear.
  • Belay devices: help control the rope while climbing.
  • Practice for the rope climbing.

Tips for Choosing Gear

  • Choose gear that fits properly.
  • Buy quality equipment from trusted brands.
  • Ask experienced climbers or staff for advice.
  • Always check gear before climbing.
